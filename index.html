Files

Degree

Term-level degree file that provides information such as campus, major, CIP code, degree awarded, and year.

Academic Year: Begins with the fall term.
Unique at the student-year-term level.

Years of Data 2010-2022
Observations 900,491
Number of Variables 15

Enrollment

Term-level enrollment file that provides demographic, background, and academic information.

Academic Year: Begins with the fall term.
Unique at the student-year-term-campus level.

Years of Data 2010-2022
Observations 10,235,144
Number of Variables 62

FinAid

Annual financial aid file that includes federal, state, and institutional aid information.

FAFSA Filing Year: One year prior the the FAFSA Award Year.
Unique at the student-year-month level.

Years of Data 2010-2022
Observations 4,114,042
Number of Variables 67
